# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Banking customers (specifically UBA Group customers like Oluwaseun) looking to manage their finances, save money, and get AI-powered financial coaching.

## Product Purpose
The Smart Money MVP serves as a modern, high-performance web dashboard that provides financial overviews, quick transaction access, and AI-driven insights to improve financial health.

## Brand Personality
Modern, minimalist, sleek, confident, and highly functional. It leverages a clean aesthetic with purposeful micro-interactions (brutalist influences but refined).

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, overwhelming legacy banking interfaces.
- Generic SaaS templates without brand identity.
- Flashy but purposeless animations (no bouncing or elastic easing).

## Design Principles
- **Modern Minimalist**: Keep the interface clean and focus on data.
- **Purposeful Motion**: Use animations (like waves or shimmers) to delight, not distract.
- **Brand Consistency**: UBA Red (#E60026) anchors the experience.
- **Frictionless Action**: Quick actions must feel instantly tactile and responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for financial numbers.
- Semantic HTML and keyboard-navigable tabs.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion for animations.
